HI THERE!
Welcome to my portfolio! I'm a rising junior from Ghana majoring in Computer Science with minors in Interactive Media and Music at New York University Abu Dhabi. I got introduced to coding in the spring semester of my freshman year and knew right then that I would love to do this for a lifetime thus my decision to major in Computer Science.

I love building software, websites and even games! My favorite thing about Computer Science is that feeling of excitement and achievement after solving a challenge. I love learning new things which means you'll often see me engaging in some new activity.

But well, coding isn't all I do. I am passionate about music and recently started playing the piano and guitar as well as DJing! I also have an expertise in sound and light engineering and have facilitated a number of events on my campus.
